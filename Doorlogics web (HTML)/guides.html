Diagnostic Logs

Common operational questions addressed by our principal engineering team.

My garage door operates with noticeable friction.

This is frequently a tension spring imbalance or depleted roller lubrication. In dusty environments, tracks require refined, silicone-based lubricants rather than standard viscous grease, which compounds the issue.

What is the lifecycle of a standard motor battery?

Under normal conditions, 12 to 18 months. However, recurrent load shedding can reduce this significantly. For longevity, we advise integrating advanced system upgrades.

Can access be granted remotely?

Absolutely. We expertly install GSM and Wi-Fi architecture, allowing you to interface with your intercom and actuate your gates from your smartphone, regardless of your location.
