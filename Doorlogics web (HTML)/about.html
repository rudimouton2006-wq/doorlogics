The Doorlogics History

Applied Experience.

Building a solid business through hands-on field expertise and uncompromising service since 1992.

Owner Managed

Knowledge that filters down to the product.

Since 1992, Doorlogics has operated from our base in Oakdale, Bellville. Owned and managed by Ronnie Mouton, we have spent decades building a solid business based not just on experience in the field, but making sure all this experience filters down to the actual products delivered.

We are repair specialists of all makes and models, and we handle the manufacturing and installation of custom-made gates and garage doors. Because of our commitment to quality and our top-class after-sales service, we now boast a very reliable and vast client base.

"We believe in reliable, honest work. Our knowledge ensures that every installation is secure, reliable, and perfectly suited to your home."
